B.Tech

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program at IIT Patna is a rigorous and innovative undergraduate course designed to develop future engineers and technology leaders. The curriculum blends strong theoretical foundations with extensive practical exposure and research opportunities, covering core engineering disciplines, emerging technologies, and essential soft skills. Students are prepared to solve real-world problems and drive technological advancements across industries.
