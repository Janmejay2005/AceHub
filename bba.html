BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at IIT Patna is designed to nurture future business leaders and entrepreneurs. The program blends foundational business knowledge with practical skills in management, finance, marketing, and analytics. Through a mix of online and on-campus learning, students gain a holistic understanding of the modern business landscape and develop the skills needed to excel in diverse industries.

BBA 101: Principles of Management BBA 102: Business Communication BBA 103: Microeconomics BBA 104: Business Mathematics
Semester 2
BBA 105: Financial Accounting BBA 106: Macroeconomics BBA 107: Business Statistics BBA 108: Organizational Behaviour
DIPLOMA MODULE
Semester 3

Semester 4
BBA 205: Financial Management BBA 206: Operations Management BBA 207: Research Methodology BBA 208: Management Information Systems
